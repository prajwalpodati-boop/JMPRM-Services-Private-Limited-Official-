About The Company

TRUSTED.
REGISTERED.
RELIABLE.

J M P R M Services Private Limited is a government-registered private transport company incorporated with the MCA on 8th March 2024, operating under the Registrar of Companies, Hyderabad.

As per MCA records, JMPRM is involved in Human Resources Provision and Management of Human Resources Functions, with an active presence in logistics and transport services across Telangana.

With 7 fleet types spanning 6-foot AC vans to 24-foot heavy containers, we serve every consignment need — urban last-mile to interstate bulk freight.
